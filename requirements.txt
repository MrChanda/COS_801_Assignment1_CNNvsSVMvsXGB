System Requirements
Platforms
OS: Windows 10/11, Ubuntu 20.04+/22.04, or macOS 13+
Python: 3.10 or 3.11 (recommended)

Hardware
CPU: x86-64 (AVX support recommended)
RAM: ≥ 8 GB (16 GB+ recommended for faster training)
Disk: ≥ 10 GB free for datasets, caches, and results
GPU (optional but recommended for CNN):
NVIDIA T4/RTX or better (Compute Capability ≥ 7.5)
Matching CUDA/cuDNN for your TensorFlow version
On Google Colab (T4 GPU) no extra driver setup is required

Network
Internet access to fetch packages/datasets (first run).

Python Dependencies
Only list top-level packages in requirements.txt. Subpackages (e.g., tensorflow.keras.layers, sklearn.metrics.pairwise) are included with their parents.
tensorflow (provides tensorflow.keras & tensorflow.keras.layers)
numpy
pandas
matplotlib
seaborn
scikit-learn (provides sklearn.pipeline, sklearn.preprocessing, sklearn.svm, sklearn.model_selection, sklearn.metrics, sklearn.metrics.pairwise)
xgboost
Standard library modules (os, math, random) require no installation.
